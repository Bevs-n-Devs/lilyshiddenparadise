Manage Your Tenants with Ease

As a landlord, managing your tenants can be a time-consuming task. But with our Tenant Dashboard, you can streamline the process and make informed decisions with ease.

Our Tenant Dashboard allows you to view and manage all tenant applications in one place, including the ability to accept or deny applications. You can also manually create new tenant profiles, setting their move-in date, monthly rent amount and currency, room type, and rent due date.

Additionally, our Tenant Dashboard gives you the ability to message available tenants directly, making it easy to communicate and stay connected. With our dashboard, you can manage your tenants with confidence and make the most of your rental properties.
